Support our Educational Empowerment Program

At SenaFoundation, we believe that education is the key to changing lives. Our Educational Empowerment Programs aim to provide learning opportunities for children, youth, and women from poor or disadvantaged backgrounds. We work hard to help them gain the knowledge, skills, and values they need to build a brighter future for themselves and their families.

What We Do

  • Scholarships and Financial Help: We offer scholarships and financial support to students who cannot afford education. This helps them stay in school and focus on learning without worrying about money.
  • Skill Development Workshops: These workshops teach practical skills like using computers, communication, and starting small businesses. This training helps people find jobs and become self-reliant.
  • Seminars and Conferences: We organize seminars where experts talk about important topics like education, careers, and personal development. This helps students, teachers, and parents stay informed and motivated.
