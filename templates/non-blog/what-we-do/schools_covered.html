Schools We Have Visited

At SenaFoundation, we believe in reaching out to schools to empower students through education and support. Here are some of the schools we have partnered with:

Govt. Higher Secondary School, Bangalore

We conducted a series of workshops focusing on career guidance and skills development for students in their final years. The feedback was overwhelmingly positive, with many students expressing newfound confidence in their future paths.

St. Joseph's School, Kalaburagi

Our team organized an interactive session on the importance of education and lifelong learning, inspiring students to pursue their dreams and aspirations.

Surathkal Public School, Surathkal

We implemented a reading program aimed at improving literacy rates among primary school students. The initiative has shown promising results, with many students demonstrating improved reading skills.

Manipal International School, Manipal

Our outreach program provided resources and training for teachers to enhance their teaching methodologies, ensuring a better learning environment for students.
