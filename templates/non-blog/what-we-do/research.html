Research on Free and Values-Based Education

At SenaFoundation, we believe that education is a fundamental right. Our research focuses on exploring innovative methods to provide free, quality education while instilling values that foster good citizenship and responsible behavior among individuals.

Our Objectives

  • To identify effective models of free education that can be implemented in diverse communities.
  • To integrate values-based education into curriculums that emphasize moral and ethical development.
  • To conduct workshops and training sessions for educators on incorporating values into teaching practices.
  • To collaborate with other organizations and educational institutions for knowledge sharing and resource pooling.

Current Research Projects

Project: Pathways to Free Education

This project explores various funding models that enable the delivery of free education to underprivileged communities. Our research team is investigating sustainable funding sources, including public-private partnerships and community-driven initiatives.

Project: Values in Education

Focusing on the integration of values-based education in schools, this project studies the impact of teaching ethical behavior and social responsibility on students' overall development and community engagement.
